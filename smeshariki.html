Smeshariki

Kikoriki, known in Russian as Smeshariki (Russian: Смеша́рики), is a Russian animated television series consisting of 208 episodes of 6 minutes and 30 seconds each, aimed at children of 3 to 8 years. The first episode premiered in Russia on May 7, 2004.

Let's meet the main Smeshariki characters:

Krosh

Blue rabbit with huge ears, the choleric person. The most part of time stays in excellent mood. It is friendly, active and purposeful, but at the same time it is inconsiderate, fussy and often interrupts the interlocutor. It is greedy for all new and unusual, easily is fond. Thirst for adventures in combination with irresponsibility, short-sightedness and indestructible optimism constantly pushes him on adventures in which he involves the Hedgehog. Can fly as a result of hypnosis. Characteristic exclamation: "Fir-trees needles!". "The engineer of a radio communication" has very good memory and a rank.

Yozhik

Crimson hedgehog with violet prickles smooth by sight. The best friend Krosha, phlegmatic person. Wears glasses. In the Lullaby for the Hedgehog series it becomes clear that he comes from Indochina hedgehogs though it hardly so. Unlike the friend, the Hedgehog is brought well up, judicious and careful. Understands when Krosh isn't right, and helps him with communication with others. The hedgehog is a little sluggish, timid, Krosha usually doesn't resist ideas. It is excessively sensitive to people around and loves when everything is quiet and peaceful. Has claustrophobia. Collects candy wrappers and cactuses, loves an order. Has ability to reconcile quarreled.

Barash

Lilac ram. Melancholiac, poet. Requires a lot of attention from people around. Often worries for all. Suffers from sleepwalking though during wakefulness is afraid of height. Inspired, hypochondriac and superstitious, believes in effectiveness of mascots. Late wakes up, and can sit up long after midnight in the evening. The constant "creative" disorder reigns in his house. Has a number of the hidden knowledge and skills which are shown unexpectedly for him: knowledge of several foreign languages, ability to knitting. In "PIN code" shows hostility to sciences and often grumbles about the fact that nothing unusual occurs. It is in love with Nyusha.

Nyusha

Female pig, sanguine person. Beautiful, harmonous and charming. Very much loves tales of princes charming, dreams to become the princess. Persistently follows the fashion. It is very curious, likes to be the focus of attention. Jealous is also the owner. Tries to be womanly, however doesn't shun to play with boys. Relations with Barash difficult: on the one hand, Nyusha is attached to him, with another — often defiantly ignores his feelings or even uses them in the purposes why they quite often quarrel.

Kar Karych

Raven with the interesting past: traveled much, acted in circus. Celebrates birthday on June 10. Has foreign roots, a real name of "von Karytsch". Artist, musician. Has ear for music, plays grand piano, a guitar, a violoncello and a violin, sings. Sanguine person. Optimist. Kar-Karych likes to tell stories from the life. Smeshariki respect him for wisdom and rich life experience, often address him for recommendation. The conjurer, the greatest achievement in career — repeating of a trick of Gudini. Can use hypnosis. Very rich imagination. Everyone trusts it their secrets.

Pin

The penguin inventor, the German, talks to strong accent. Sometimes swears in German. Talented inventor and designer. Sanguine person. Catholic. Sleeps in the refrigerator. In the movie "Smeshariki. The beginning" I was a security guard in the museum. Has created many robots, including the Iron nurse and Bibi. Favourite exclamations — "The Compression!" and "Oh mein Gott!". In Pin's house always chaos, but Pin tries to eliminate him. In honor of Ping the cartoon serial PIN code has been called. Has a hangar and a big useful dump. The dump has come from the airship on which Smeshariki from the megalopolis flew.

Sovunya

Violet owl. Was a games-master earlier, adores sport, loves fresh air. Sanguine person. Understands medicine, at Smeshariki carries out functions of the doctor and the nurse. Thinks of health of all Smeshariki much, constantly agitates them for a healthy lifestyle and exercises. Very much the hozyaystvenna, keeps the house clean and an order, does procurements for the winter, bakes excellent pies, possesses a world record on preparation of the biggest cake in the world. It is pragmatic, possesses wide life experience. It is quite sentimental in this connection takes everything very much to heart. In life it is a little disseminated. By means of a glass sphere can predict future, trusts in the predictions though they not always come true. Is able to drive cars.

Losyash

Elk scientist; seriously goes in for astronomy, biology, chemistry, physics, is interested also in other sciences, including has the Nobel prize for research about communication of length of horns with intelligence. Realist. Denies everything that contradicts laws of science or it is not possible to explain from the scientific point of view. Has complex because of too small, from his point of view, horns. The most smart of Smeshariki, likes to read books. Sanguine person. Ambidexter. Has huge library. In the house, as well as at Barash, "the working disorder", however has to it the complete antithesis. Works at the computer, often plays computer games "The prince for Nyusha 2" and "Olimpiarik: Who's the first". Has a clone — the negative character whom I have created. It is unreceptive to hypnosis. In youth has been expelled from institute of inventors for discovery of inert gases which I could not think up application. I have twice salvaged Earth from plutonets, has from them two medals and a rank "Hero of Pluto". Favourite exclamation: "Phenomenally!", characteristic address to other Smeshariki: "My friends!" with addition of determination, suitable to a case.

Kopatych

Brown bear, has a kitchen garden where grows up fruit and vegetables for all Smeshariki. In difficult situations takes everything under the control, амбиверт, doesn't love entertainments and holidays, but is a furious fan, regardless of a sport. Falls into hibernation in the winter, though not always. His father considers gardening and other "peaceful" occupations unworthy "the real bear" therefore Kopatych carefully hides from it the vital hobbies. Orthodox Christian. There are a sister and the niece Stepanida (Pandi, Stesha). Favourite exclamation — "Bite me a bee!". Sometimes speaks "Hurt me a bee!" or "Butt me a bee!".

Bibi

The robot made by Pin. Pin considers him the son, Bibi considers Pin the father. Bibi isn't able to speak properly, only signals, but at the same time quite reasonable and competent. Spends the most part of time somewhere in space, being engaged in own affairs, but from time to time visits Smeshariki and sends Pin photos and letters. During short "earth" vacation with pleasure plays with "children's" part of Smeshariki who too very much love it.
